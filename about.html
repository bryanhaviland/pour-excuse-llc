Who We Are

Built by parents who were tired of guessing.

The story behind Pour Excuse LLC, and why we build what we build.

Pour Excuse LLC started with a simple frustration: too much of a parent's limited free time gets wasted hunting for information that should already be easy to find — whether that's scrambling for a last-minute substitute player, trying to figure out if a field is worth the drive, or just looking for something genuinely worth doing with an hour to spare.

We build small, focused apps and media that solve those problems directly, without the clutter. Our goal isn't to take up more of your time — it's to give some of it back.

Every product we ship starts the same way: a real parent, dealing with a real headache, deciding there had to be a better way. If it doesn't solve an actual problem, we don't build it.
